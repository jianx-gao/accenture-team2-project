# Product Overview

Event planning platform addressing logistics challenges for large-scale events (sports finals, concerts, weddings).

## Target Users
- Event planners and organizers
- Venue managers
- City operations coordinators

## Core Problems Addressed
- Budget constraints and cost management
- Schedule delays and timeline coordination
- Communication gaps between stakeholders
- Sustainable practice integration
- Complex logistics management across venues, attendees, and city operations

## Competitive Context
Competing with established platforms: Cvent, Eventbrite, Bizzabo, Zola
